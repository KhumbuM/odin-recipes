Xigugu

Description

Xigugu is a traditional Vatsonga food. It can be used as snack but in historical times it was used as carry food for travellers when they went on long journeys that could last months. This was so because it is highly nutritious and does not spoil. The only catch is you will need to have readily available sources of water to keep your body rehydrated.

It is mainly made from crushed roasted corn and crushed roasted peanuts.

Ingridients

  • 3 cups of maize meal
  • 5 cups of peanuts
  • 1 spoon of salt

Instructions

  1. Dry roast peanuts until golden to chocolate brown. Let the roasted peanuts cool down overnight
  2. Grind roasted peanuts to fine peanut powder
  3. Separately dry roast maize corn and let it cool off.
  4. Mix maize, peanut powder and salt. Grind mix on traditional wooden crusher until mixture becomes an oily paste
  5. It is now ready to be served. It can be enjoyed with sprinle of sugar but thta is optional.
