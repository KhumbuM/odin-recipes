Tihove

Description

Tihove is a traditional Vatsonga dish. Main ingredients are samp, beans and crushed peanuts. The are various ways of preparing it.

Some people like it plain with beans. Others enjoy it with crushed peanuts. It can be eaten on its own but can also be enjoyed with beef stew.

Ingridients

  • 3 cups samp
  • 1 lt hot water
  • pinch of salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup crushed raw peanuts
  • 1/2 cup of groundnuts
  • 1/2 cup of traditional beans

Instructions

  1. Wash beans and samp separately
  2. Soak for 15 minutes. Skim off any debris that floats to the top
  3. Boil the samp in a lt of water until samp is cooked (2 1/2 hours). Add additional water as required
  4. Boil beans in 2 cups of boiling water until soft. Season with salt and set aside
  5. Once samp is cooked, add beans and crushed peanuts and groundnuts. Stir until combined
  6. Reduce and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes
  7. Serve with beef stew
